The Cheeto Story:

At the risk of losing the street-cred I don't have, I shall impart of the tale of the time in
middle school I stuck a flaming hot cheeto up my nose. Don't worry, it gets worse with context.

You see, one of the fine young lads I sat at lunch with challenged me one day. He said that I Was
far to proper, squarish, and boring to do ever do something fun. To retort, I offered him to beqeuth
before me a task which an individual such as himself would have no trouble doing as part of a classical
jest. Feeling smarmy, he proclamined that I could never stick Chester Cheetah's hotest potato
product down my nasal orifice.

Not wanting to be outdone by such a trivial task, I quickly grabbed a long, thin piece
and began to squiggle it up my nasal cavity. One could say the game was heated at that
point, but despite the heat, the deed was done no problem and my table-peers stood corrected.
The problem came when I attempted to remove the wretched sustanence, as it promptly broke in half and
left a piece stuck beyond the bend of my nostril. There was nothing to panic about though, I could still
breathe perfectly fine and cheetos are mostly carbs, which would eventually salivate and breakdown
without interaction, and I knew this well. However, constantly sneezing red mucus for two days was
much less fun.

Funnily enough, the lad who gave such a quest in the first place himself was to squemish to follow
up my performance, as he feared the same fate would befall him. The table promptly called him out
on it, and I left lunch having won something. Not respect, but something.
